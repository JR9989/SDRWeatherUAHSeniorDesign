1001 AM CDT Mon Apr 24 2023

...The Flood Warning is extended for the following rivers in 
Minnesota...

  Buffalo River near Dilworth affecting Clay County.

.Red River basin river flooding to continues. New snowmelt will 
result in a slow, gradual recession of the Buffalo River near 
Dilworth. The river is expected to fall into Action stage by mid 
this week.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Please report observed flooding to local emergency services or law 
enforcement and request they pass this information to the National 
Weather Service when you can do so safely.

Safety message - Turn around, don't drown. Don't travel through 
flooded areas. Most flood deaths occur in vehicles.
