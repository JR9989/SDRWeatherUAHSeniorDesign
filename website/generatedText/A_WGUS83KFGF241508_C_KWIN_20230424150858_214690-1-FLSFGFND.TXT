...The Flood Warning continues for the following rivers in 
Minnesota...

  Wild Rice River (MN) at Hendrum affecting Norman County.

.Red River basin river flooding to continues. Recent snowmelt will 
result in slower gradual recession of the Wild Rice River at 
Hendrum. The river is expected to remain within Minor Flood stage 
through the forecast period.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Please report observed flooding to local emergency services or law 
enforcement and request they pass this information to the National 
Weather Service when you can do so safely.

Safety message - Turn around, don't drown. Don't travel through 
flooded areas. Most flood deaths occur in vehicles.
